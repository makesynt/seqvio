# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- giving actionable technical feedback
- assuming good intent
- discussing disagreements on the merits
- helping newcomers understand current repo constraints

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- doxxing or threats
- derailing technical threads with hostility or spam

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, or pull requests that violate these standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainers through the repository owners or moderators using a private channel when available.
